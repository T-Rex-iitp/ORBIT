# Example queries for testing the text-to-SQL system
# Each line is a separate query (lines starting with # are comments)

# Basic queries
Show me all flights
What are the latest 10 flights?
List all aircraft callsigns

# Altitude queries
Show me flights above 10000 feet
Which flights are flying below 5000 feet altitude?
Find aircraft with altitude between 20000 and 30000

# Speed queries
Show me the fastest flights
Which flights have ground speed over 400 knots?
List flights with ground speed less than 200

# Location queries
Show me flights near latitude 37 and longitude 127
Which aircraft are in Seoul area?
Find flights within 1 degree of latitude 35

# Callsign specific queries
Show me information about flight KAL123
Where is Korean Air 851?
Track the position of Asiana 214

# Time-based queries
Show me flights from the last hour
What flights were recorded today?
Display the most recent flight positions

# Emergency and special conditions
Are there any emergency flights?
Show me aircraft that are on the ground
Which flights have emergency status?

# Statistical queries
What is the average altitude of all flights?
Count how many flights are currently tracked
Show me the highest and lowest altitudes

# Complex queries
Show me the top 5 flights by altitude with their callsigns and ground speed
Find flights above 30000 feet that are flying faster than 450 knots
List all Korean Air flights with their current positions and altitudes
